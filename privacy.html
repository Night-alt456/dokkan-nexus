Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 25, 2026

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what data Dokkan Nexus collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described here.
01

What Data We Collect

Dokkan Nexus collects only the minimum data necessary to operate its features:

  • Discord User ID — used to associate community team submissions with your account and manage deletion permissions.
  • Discord Username — displayed alongside community team submissions so others can credit the contributor.
  • Discord Server Name — displayed alongside team submissions to indicate which server the submission originated from.
  • Community Team Submissions — the team data you voluntarily submit via /submitteam, including card selections, event, stage, and notes.
02

What We Do NOT Collect

Dokkan Nexus does not collect message content, passwords, email addresses, payment information, or any personal information beyond what is listed above.

The Bot does not monitor or log general Discord messages. It only processes data when a slash command is explicitly used.

03

How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Display community team submissions to other users across servers.
  • Allow you to manage and delete your own submissions.
  • Send moderation notifications (e.g. if a submission is removed by a moderator).

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

04

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the server hosting Dokkan Nexus. We take reasonable precautions to protect stored data, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data is retained for as long as your submissions exist. Deleting your submissions via /deleteteam removes your data from the database.

05

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your own community team submissions at any time using /deleteteam.
  • Request deletion of all data associated with your Discord User ID by contacting us directly.
  • Know what data is stored about you upon request.
06

Third-Party Services

Dokkan Nexus sources card data from the Dokkan Battle Fandom Wiki. No user data is shared with or transmitted to the wiki or any other third-party service.

The Bot operates within Discord's platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.

07

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Where possible, significant changes will be announced.

08

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact us via GitHub at github.com/Night-alt456.
